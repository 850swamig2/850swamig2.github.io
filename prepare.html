How to Prepare For Your Koleoso Consultation

Come to divination with respect, clear questions, humility, and readiness to receive instruction, correction, and spiritual next steps.

Before the Session

  • Write down up to three clear questions.
  • Sit quietly and reflect on why you are seeking divination now.
  • Avoid approaching the oracle casually or with testing energy.
  • Be ready to receive ebo, prayer, offering, devotional practice, or correction if indicated.
  • Have something available to take notes.

Best Questions to Ask

  • What must I understand about this situation?
  • What is the spiritual condition around this matter?
  • What correction, offering, discipline, or action is required?
  • What is my next step for destiny alignment?
  • Is initiation, training, or deeper devotional work appropriate?
Session Flow

Ifá Reveals the Pattern. Tehuti Reveals the Meaning.

The session may include opening prayer, ancestor acknowledgment, Ifá divination, Khemetic Tehuti illumination, counsel, ebo guidance, and next steps for spiritual development.

Opening

Prayer, ancestor acknowledgment, and confirmation of the client’s main question.

Divination

Ifá and Tehuti are consulted to reveal sacred pattern and divine meaning.

Counsel

The guidance is interpreted into practical spiritual instruction and next steps.
