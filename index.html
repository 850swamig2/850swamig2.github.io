Is the divination intake the same as the Institute application?

No. The divination intake requests a Koleoso consultation. The Institute application requests review for SGI education, training, program placement, or initiation-readiness pathways.

Does an Institute application guarantee acceptance or initiation?

No. Applications are reviewed, and initiation or major spiritual pathways must be confirmed through divination before commencing.

Is Koleoso fortune-telling?

No. It is framed as sacred consultation, spiritual diagnosis, character correction, destiny alignment, and guidance for proper action.

The Institute application is submitted through a Cloudflare Pages Function and saved in the bound D1 database for authorized review and follow-up.
