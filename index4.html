Sacred Consultation

What Is a Babalawo Divination Service?

A Babalawo divination service is a structured consultation with an Ifá priest who approaches the oracle for spiritual diagnosis, clarification, correction, and guidance. It is not designed merely to predict events. The purpose is to understand the condition surrounding a concern and determine what wisdom, discipline, prayer, offering, ebo, or practical action may restore alignment.

Ifá Divination

Ifá may reveal the governing Odu, supporting spiritual forces, Orisha influence, blessings, obstructions, obligations, and corrective instruction connected with the client's question.

Khemetic Tehuti Consultation

Tehuti consultation provides an additional lens for sacred law, divine intelligence, ancestral memory, incarnation purpose, correspondence, meaning, and the lesson contained within the client's present circumstances.

This Babalawo divination service asks: What is present? Why is it present? What must be understood? and What action restores right relationship with destiny?

Questions for the Oracle

How to Frame a Serious Divination Question

Helpful Questions

  • What spiritual condition surrounds this situation?
  • What must I understand before making this decision?
  • What is blocking progress, and what correction is required?
  • What action would place me in better alignment with destiny?
  • Is further spiritual training or initiation appropriate now?

Approach the Oracle Respectfully

Ask what you genuinely need to understand, change, strengthen, or decide. Avoid testing questions or attempts to force a preferred answer. Divination is most useful when the client is prepared to receive truth, responsibility, and corrective instruction.

Spiritual divination does not replace medical, legal, financial, or mental-health care from a qualified professional.
Frequently Asked Questions

Before You Request a Babalawo Consultation

What does a Babalawo do during divination?

A Babalawo approaches Ifá on behalf of the client, identifies the revealed spiritual pattern, and interprets its counsel in relation to the client's concern. The reading may include Odu, Orisha, blessing or obstruction, spiritual obligation, and corrective action.

Is this service only an Ifá reading?

Koleoso combines Ifá divination with Khemetic Tehuti consultation. Ifá reveals sacred pattern, while Tehuti provides an additional framework for meaning, correspondence, divine intelligence, and ancestral understanding.

Can I receive a Babalawo consultation remotely?

Yes. The intake, payment, preparation, scheduling, and consultation pathway is designed to support remote clients. Session arrangements may include telephone or Zoom according to availability.

Can divination confirm initiation readiness?

Divination may help determine whether initiation, training, devotional work, or another spiritual step should be explored. All initiations should be confirmed by divination before commencing.

Will ebo always be recommended?

Not every situation requires the same response. When indicated, guidance may include ebo, prayer, offering, sacrifice, devotional practice, behavioral correction, restitution, or another action appropriate to the revealed condition.

How do I begin?

Complete the secure intake with your background, main concern, and up to three questions. Retain the confirmation number, then proceed to payment and scheduling.
